How it Works

Are you bored with nothing to do in your area? Need people to do something with? Or perhaps you are looking to try something new and make new friends at the same time.

Eventabase is an events and activities advertiser and organiser, that allows its registered users to both advertise an event or activity they wish to host, as well as search for activities or events hosted by other users, to potentially participate in.

To advertise an event/activity all a user has to do is provide the required information. Other users then can register their interest, and the host can choose who to invite. Any further communication between the host and its attendees is managed through the message dashboard.

Users can search for events/activities they like, by location and using keywords. All users can provide reviews/ratings for other users as participants and hosts independently.
